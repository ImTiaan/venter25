Platform: What Venter Will Deliver

Business Growth

Cut red tape. Seed micro-grants. Make Roxwood the easiest place to start a shop—mechanics, food stalls, and trades that keep money local.

Accountable Policing

Community-based policing that answers to Roxwood residents. Transparent oversight and public reporting—safety without overreach.

Skills & Jobs

Open community training hubs for trades, apprenticeships with local businesses, and youth programs that create real careers on the island.

Roxwood First: 100-Day Plan

1) Local Economy

  • Fast-track permits for island businesses.
  • “Roxwood Buys Roxwood” procurement rules for docks & city contracts.
  • Micro-grants for startups in mechanics, food, and trades.

2) Safe Streets

  • Community oversight board for policing.
  • Quarterly public safety reports & town halls.
  • Focus on prevention, not intimidation.

3) Skills & Youth

  • Launch the Roxwood Skills Center (free evening classes).
  • Paid apprenticeships with island businesses.
  • Youth sport & arts micro-funds.

Measure What Matters

Jobs created, permits issued, crimes solved, students trained. Results reported publicly every 100 days.
